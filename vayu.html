Modular Multi-Effect Rack

Shape Motion Into Sound

Vayu is a sleek effect rack for sound design and mixing. It blends five-band EQ, modulation, delay, reverb, stereo shaping, distortion, and compression into one visual workflow with drag-to-reorder processing and standalone playback.

Identity

Air, Depth, Movement

What Vayu Includes

Five-Band EQ

Shape lows, mids, and highs with a responsive visual curve and real-time analyzer feedback.

Modular Rack

Change effect order by dragging modules across the rack and save complete processing states.

Eight Effects

Distortion, chorus, flanger, phaser, delay, stereo, reverb, and compressor in one chain.

Preset Workflow

Use factory starting points, save your own rack presets, and store effect-specific patches for quick recall.

Standalone + Plugin

Use Vayu on the desktop or as a plugin in compatible hosts through AU and VST3 releases.

Built For Atmosphere

From subtle glue and width to motion-heavy textures, Vayu is designed for colour, movement, and space.

Installation

Standalone

Move Vayu.app into Applications and launch it like any normal macOS app.

AU

Copy Vayu.component into ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components and rescan plugins in your DAW.

VST3

Copy Vayu.vst3 into ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3 and rescan plugins in your DAW.

Official Builds

Vayu is meant to be downloaded and installed directly. End users do not need JUCE, Xcode, or source tools.
