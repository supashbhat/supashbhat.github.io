Vayu

Shape Motion Into Sound

Vayu is a modular effect rack built for fast sound design. It combines EQ, modulation, delay, reverb, distortion, stereo imaging, and compression into one visual plugin with drag-to-reorder processing and standalone playback.

Install And Use

What Vayu Includes

Five-Band EQ

Shape lows, mids, and highs with a responsive visual curve and real-time analyzer feedback.

Modular Rack

Drag effect modules to change processing order and save full rack states as presets.

Eight Effects

Distortion, chorus, flanger, phaser, delay, stereo, reverb, and compressor in one chain.

Standalone + Plugin

Use Vayu as a standalone desktop app or install it as AU and VST3 in your DAW.

Preset Workflow

Load factory starting points, save your own rack states, and keep effect-level patches ready for repeat sessions.

Built For Atmosphere

From subtle width and glue to motion-heavy textures, Vayu is designed for colour, movement, and space.

Installation

Standalone

Move Vayu.app into Applications and launch it like any normal macOS app.

AU

Copy Vayu.component into ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components and rescan plugins in your DAW.

VST3

Copy Vayu.vst3 into ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3 and rescan plugins in your DAW.

Official Builds

Vayu is meant to be downloaded and installed directly. End users do not need JUCE, CMake, or source setup.
