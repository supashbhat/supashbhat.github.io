About the researcher

Supash Bhat Music 170 Professor Davies

This archive extends an academic project on Venda Malombo, possession, trance, and modern South African performance. It is designed to grow into a fuller media-rich research environment.

The site frames research as public scholarship: not just a paper translated into HTML, but a modular archive where listening, theory, and historical context can remain visibly connected.

Ongoing research statement

Future development can add licensed photographs, more embedded recordings, expanded annotations, and a fuller comparison between ritual practice and urban festival circulation. The archive structure is intentionally modular so new findings can be added page by page.

Expansion Plan

The next natural additions would be more primary-source media, closer timestamped listening notes, and expanded work on Tabane’s refusal of the jazz label as both aesthetic and political position.
